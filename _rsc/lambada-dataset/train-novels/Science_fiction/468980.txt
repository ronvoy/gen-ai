 night time waking 3 a.m. is when i do all my best thinking . 
 it 's dark so there 's less to distract me , the air is so still i can hear the soothing hum of my blood whirling past my ears , and the four hours ' sleep i 've already had means the chaos of the day is forgotten , or at least partly processed by my subconscious .

 i did it !
