 chapter one ilona cameron 's long , caramel coloured hair trailed over the pillows , the waves and curls interwoven in a tangled mass . 
 her restless night was further evidenced by the crumpled sheets and the heaped mound that was the duvet in the middle of the bed .

 she 'd thought she was getting over him but the dream had been so vivid , bringing all those hurtful emotions back into sharp focus just when she was managing , for most of the time at least , to keep them buried .
