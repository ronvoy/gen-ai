 love conquers all ! 
 copyright by alex johnson 2013 smashwords edition foreword this is an unusual romance . 
 taking place during world war ii it contains the vagaries of wartime . 
 it is the story of a young country girl caught up in the excitement of war and falling in love with a shy young soldier . 
 unfortunately , service life leads to all kinds of surprises , and parting is one of them , so the girl is left bewildered . 
 life however can change rapidly and once again an army occurrence gives hope and a chance meeting leads the story along to a happy ending .

 attending grammar school meant leaving home at 7.20 am and arriving home at 5.30 pm .
