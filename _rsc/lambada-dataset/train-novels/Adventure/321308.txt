 *** twenty-six claire looked up at the glorious building , set in parkland , and smiled .

 *** twenty-seven claire curled over towards the window and buried her nose in the pillow .

 4a . 
 m .

 josh 's personality and anthony 's availability .

 `` come the heck on , girls .

 surely you 're not packing now , if you 're leaving today ?

 one by one , the five women slid , climbed or fell from their beds and began rummaging in plastic bags until claire thought she might scream .

 *** twenty-eight `` bloody hell ! ''
