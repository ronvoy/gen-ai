 ***** vain glory ***** chapter 1 `` call of duty '' the mystic confederacy was the civilization of a united great race and the oldest of the six galactic powers .

 vain glory was a female of the mystic race .

 `` good .

 the galactic war was raging . 
 vain glory and her new companions were on a quest to thwart the phantoms , people of the third eye . 
 the phantom army was swift and terrible , massacring every village , town and city it passed through .

 we must even if we are never to return . '' 
 nefarious good , vain glory and the priestess moody serenity all nodded their acceptance of the danger . 
 numinous was a planet between its yellow sun of the day and its smaller and cooler red sun of the night .

 the phantoms hoped to take numinous , to use it as a base to invade three other states and a colony world .
