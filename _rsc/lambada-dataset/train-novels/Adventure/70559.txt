 then again , maybe it was ... welcome to paragon city just up the rhode island coast from point judith sits the port city of paragon . 
 like all major cities it had its humble beginnings in the seventeenth century as the settlers came to the new land . 
 the natural large , deep-water harbor made it an idea port city .
