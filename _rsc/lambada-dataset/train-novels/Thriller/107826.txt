 ~ albert einstein the intensity of the cold surprised toby as much as his sudden and painful arrival -- face down in a layer of feathery snow too thin to cushion him from the hard-pack beneath it . 
 he came up sputtering and brushing frantically at the frozen powder sticking to his nose and cheeks .

 suddenly motionless , he ground his teeth and waited quietly for the shiver to subside .

 he had no idea the translation effect would leave him so wobbly .
