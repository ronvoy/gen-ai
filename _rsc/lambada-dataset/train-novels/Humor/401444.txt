 engineer ivor was a short man , somewhere in his late fifties , with a ruddy red face and the look of someone who had just been caught unexpectedly in a ferocious wind-storm . 
 his playful wisps of thinning hair had ambitions of becoming comedy performers and enjoyed nothing more than creating risqué shapes on the polished stage of his pinking scalp .

 he came from a village in the far north of scotland .
