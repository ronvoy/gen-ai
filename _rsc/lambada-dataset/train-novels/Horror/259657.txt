 prologue if alvin ever found the damn dog , he was going to kill it . 
 well , probably not , since the sorry excuse for a canine had set him back three-hundred dollars , but the thought was appealing , nonetheless . 
 it was well past lunch time , and alvin was not a man known for missing meals . 
 he had never been able to understand how some people could become so involved in an activity that they would skip a feeding . 
 he 'd worked with guys like that , back before he 'd gotten his disability , and he had never trusted them .

 hunting came in right below eating on alvin 's list , particularly coon hunting . 
 there was nothing finer than the sound of a hound baying as it treed its quarry .

 damn dog .

 not tonight .

 maybe someone had come across blue and realized that alvin would be close by .

 no , that was n't right . 
 alvin had heard the pain in the dog 's yelp . 
 none of his buddies would have done that to another man 's hunting dog . 
 if someone was out there , they must have taken care of blue , and now they were after him . 
 alvin could n't even begin to imagine who might do such a thing , but if the nut had not fallen from a tree ... he was hit again , this time on the cheekbone , just under his left eye .

 alvin lowered his gun , not wanting to accidently shoot some rugrat .
